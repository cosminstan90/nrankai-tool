What is llms.txt?

A /llms.txt file is a Markdown document placed at your website root that helps large language models efficiently discover and understand your content. It lists your most important pages with brief descriptions, organised into logical sections.

📋 Format

Markdown with H1 title, blockquote summary, and H2-sectioned resource lists

🤖 Purpose

Lets AI assistants answer questions about your site accurately without scraping every page

📍 Placement

Upload as llms.txt to your domain root — accessible at https://yourdomain.com/llms.txt
