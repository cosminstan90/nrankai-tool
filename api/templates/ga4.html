How to export from Google Analytics 4:

  1. Open Google Analytics 4 → select your property
  2. Go to Reports → Engagement → Pages and screens
  3. Set your date range (last 3 months recommended)
  4. Click Export → Download CSV — this gives you the pages report
  5. Go to Reports → Acquisition → Traffic acquisition
  6. Export again for the channel groups report
  7. Come back here, create a property, and upload both CSV files
